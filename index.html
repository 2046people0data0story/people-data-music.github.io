ABOUT ME

I believe in the power of sound

Singing in 4 languages and having explored 4 continents, I discovered the universal human need to listen and be heard. The more people I share music with, the more confident I am in its power in helping us find a common ground in this chaotic world.

Engaging technology and analytics, I wish to promote the voice of the vulnerable and curate audio experiences that most resonate with our authentic values.
